BMI Calculator

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a number that helps you see if you're at a healthy weight for your height. A BMI calculator is a tool that helps you determine your Body Mass Index (BMI) by inputting your weight and height.

BMI Calculator Chart

BMI Weight Status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Healthy
25.5 - 29.9 Overweight
30.0 - and Above Obese

Calculate Your BMI

You calculate it by dividing your weight by your height squared. It helps categorize you into underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese, but doesn't measure body fat directly.
